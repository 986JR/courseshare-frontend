About CourseShare

A community-driven platform for sharing academic resources and empowering learning.

Our Mission

CourseShare is a digital academic resource hub designed to help students access and share study materials effortlessly.

We believe learning becomes stronger when knowledge flows freely from student to student, class to class, and generation to generation.

Our Vision

To become the leading academic resource-sharing platform in Africa connecting learners with knowledge, one upload at a time.

Who We Serve

  • University students
  • College students
  • Alumni who still want to learn & contribute
  • Anyone seeking academic notes, assignments, books, past papers, and guides

Why We Exist

Students struggle to find proper study notes especially during exams or when switching devices.

WhatsApp groups get messy, files get lost, alumni leave, years pass and knowledge disappears. CourseShare fixes that.

We store resources centrally, neatly, searchable and forever accessible.

What We Offer

Course Materials

Notes, slides, assignments, past papers, and more.

Filtered & Searchable

No more searching blindly. Find exactly what you need.

Reviews & Comments

Learn from others before you download the resources.

Ratings System

Best materials rise to the top through community rating.

Community Collaboration

Everyone can easily contribute to the growing library.

Our Value Statement

Open Learning

Knowledge should be accessible, not hidden.

Collaboration

Students learn better when they share.

Empowerment

Good notes = better grades = better futures.

Continuous Growth

Alumni knowledge helps future students.

Our Journey

CourseShare started as a simple idea among students who wanted easy access to shared materials. It grew into a platform built with passion, code, and a vision for a smarter academic community.
